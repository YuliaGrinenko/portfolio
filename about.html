About Me

Hi! I’m Yulia, a front-end developer originally from Ukraine, now based in the United Kingdom.

My coding journey began while I was taking care of my young child. I started an online course, using every free minute, and it quickly grew into a passion. I completed a front-end development course at SheCodes Academy, where I built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

After that, I continued my learning through self-study, completing a JavaScript course at Codecademy and working on personal projects to further sharpen my skills.

Before diving into development, I worked for 8 years in HR at an IT company, collaborating closely with developers and IT managers. This experience gave me a unique insight into the tech industry, and it’s one of the reasons I felt drawn to coding.

Now, as I embark on my journey as a front-end developer, I’m excited to combine my background with my new skills to create intuitive and engaging web experiences.
